IMAGE REPLACEMENT NOTES

To replace images, keep the exact same filenames and folder:
Final website/Images/

Important fixed paths:
- Desktop hero image: Images/Hero image2.jpg
- Mobile hero image: Images/Hero image4.jpg
- Desktop popup image: Images/Slide3_2.jpg
- Mobile popup image: Images/Popup-mobile.jpg

Do not add another .jpg at the end.
Use exact uppercase/lowercase spelling. On hosting, Slide1_1.jpg and slide1_1.jpg are different files.
Avoid spaces in filenames used inside srcset. Use Popup-mobile.jpg instead of Popup mobile.jpg.

After replacing images, hard refresh the browser or clear cache.
